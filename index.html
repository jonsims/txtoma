Supercharger Route Card · Return Trip

Austin → Needham

Chargers chosen as destinations, not waypoints the car picks. Fewer, better stops — shade, seating off the ground, a walkable carry-out, and grass to walk Luna away from the crowd.

🚗 Model Y Long Range 3 days · Jul 9–11 ≈2,005 mi Christina + Luna 🐕 Plan legs ~230 mi

Last trip stacked up too many stops, each chosen for the fewest charging minutes, at exposed strip-mall chargers with nowhere good to be with a reactive dog in the July heat. This time the plan anchors each day on one genuinely pleasant place and keeps everything else short. Two overnight plugs shape the whole thing.

◐ Little Rock · Level 1 only

Arrive ~80%, wake at ~95%

A 120V plug adds just ~3–4 mi/hr → ~45–55 mi overnight. So Day 1's final stop tops up only enough to arrive ~80%. Chasing a true 100% would mean arriving ~85% — not worth it; ~95% covers Day 2's first leg easily.

⚡ Check, VA · Level 2

Arrive low, leave full

~25–30 mi/hr recovers a full charge overnight regardless of arrival state. No last-stop constraint on Day 2 — roll in low, leave Day 3 at 100%.

Battery percentages on each stop are planning estimates for a Model Y LR in July heat — confirm the exact numbers with an ABRP run before you leave.

The tradeoff · 3 vs 4 charges

Recommended — 4 charges: Jackson → Cookeville → Knoxville splash → Bristol/Abingdon. No leg over ~130 mi after Jackson; the splash de-risks the mountains and barely costs time since Jackson is a long lunch anyway.

Lean — 3 charges: drop Knoxville, but Cookeville→Bristol becomes ~215 mi through the mountains — leave Cookeville at 90%+ (a long session at a slow 150 kW site) and watch consumption.

The tradeoff · 3 vs 4 charges

Recommended — 3 charges: Winchester → Clinton → Sturbridge. Spacing 215 / 195 / 190 / 55 mi — every leg under 230 with buffer, all three 250 kW V3. Clean.

Conservative — add a 4th: a short splash at Southington, CT (Target, 250 kW) splits the Clinton→Sturbridge leg if the hilly, traffic-heavy CT stretch leaves you wanting cushion. Fast insurance, not a relax stop — save that for Sturbridge.
